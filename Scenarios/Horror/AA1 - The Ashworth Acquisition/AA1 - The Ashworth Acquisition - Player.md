**Scenario:** The Ashworth Acquisition
**Genre / Suggested Lens:** Horror / Cosmic (Horror's signature lens)
**Built for Core Engine:** v1.0

## Premise

Whitmore University's Special Collections archive has had a bad month. Three weeks ago, a first-year grad student named Desmond Marsh — cataloguing the newly donated Ashworth estate collection — was found in the east stacks at 3 AM, catatonic, murmuring the same six words on a loop: *"It reads you back."* Nobody's figured out what that means. Since then, pages have gone missing from books that were fine the week before, the after-hours badge log shows the head archivist, Dr. Voss, coming in at hours the building's supposed to be empty, and every appointment on her calendar has been canceled without a word of explanation. A worried colleague has asked the hero to look into it quietly — before the university's PR problem becomes something worse.

## Setting

Narrow stacks, climate-controlled vaults, the smell of old paper and cold ozone. An elevator that groans longer than it should between floors. A restricted reading room at the back of the east stacks that requires Dr. Voss's own badge to enter — and lately, nobody's seen inside it.

## Suggested Genre & Lens

Horror. Written for the **Cosmic** lens (Horror's signature, default lens), but portable to **Gothic/Supernatural** with minor reskinning if you'd rather the threat feel more like a haunting than an incomprehensible presence.

## The Problem (What You're Told)

A grad student went catatonic in the stacks and nobody knows why. The head archivist has gone reclusive and erratic, working alone after hours in a room she won't let anyone else into. Students have started avoiding the east stacks after dark without quite being able to say why. A security guard quit without notice. Something about the newest big donation — the "Ashworth acquisition" — set all of this off, but nobody official is saying so out loud.

## Suggested Drive & Bond Hooks

- **Drive:** *Find out what happened to Desmond Marsh* — or, more broadly, *Uncover what Dr. Voss found.*
- **Bond:** A grad-student friend of Desmond's, frightened and pushing for someone to actually investigate.
- **Bond:** Dr. Voss herself, if the hero has history with her — a former mentor, now unreachable.

If you're bringing an existing character instead of building one for this hook, any Drive or Bond involving uncovering a hidden truth, a missing or changed person, or a debt to an academic mentor slots in naturally — the AI can help you fold it in during Session Zero.

## Tone & Safety Notes

Themes include obsession, loss of self, and the horror of being genuinely unable to trust your own mind or memory. If depictions of catatonia, mental unraveling, or academic/institutional cover-up are sensitive for you, raise it as a line or veil at Session Zero — the tone can flex without losing the hook.
