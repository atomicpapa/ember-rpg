# EMBER — Horror

**Version:** 1.0 — see `CHANGELOG.md`

*A Genre Pack for EMBER. Paste this together with `EMBER — Core Engine.md`. Uses only Core rules — no new subsystems.*

**Tone & premise:** The ordinary world with something terribly wrong underneath it. Characters are usually not warriors but people — investigators, professors, survivors — pulled into events that erode their safety, their certainty, and their minds. In Horror, winning often means *surviving* and *understanding*, not conquering.

**Default dials:** Lethality 2 · Magic/Tech 1 · Weirdness 3. Grittier starting array (**+2, +1, 0, 0, −1**). **Resolve** is your lifeline — it's your sanity.

**Running Horror well:** lean hard on the Core safety tools — set lines & veils at Session Zero and check in on tone. Telegraph dread before harm. Use **Conditions** like *Afraid* and *Shaken* generously, and let **Resolve** stand in for sanity. Scars here are often *psychological* (Nagging, until-healed): nightmares, a tremor, a thing they can't stop seeing.

**Coming apart (the Unraveling Clock):** track a character's fraying sanity with a personal 6-segment **Clock** named *"Unraveling"* — plain Core machinery, no new subsystem. Tick it each time the character **misses a Resolve roll against the uncanny**. When it fills, they take a **psychological Scar** (Nagging or Crippling, per how bad the exposure was) and the Clock resets to empty. This makes "coming apart" a concrete, trackable state instead of GM feel — track it in the Save file alongside other active Clocks.

## Archetype menu

Each archetype lists a stat emphasis and signature moves; new characters begin with the **starred** move.

**Investigator** — *emphasize Wits.*
- ★ **Follow the Thread.** When you dig into a mystery — records, clues, questioning — on a 10+ you uncover a solid lead *and* something you can use; on 7–9 you learn something, but it draws notice or unsettles you (mark Shaken).
- **Cool Under Fire.** Once per scene, ignore the −1 from one fear-based Condition.

**Professor** — *emphasize Wits.*
- ★ **Forbidden Knowledge.** When you consult your expertise about something uncanny, ask the GM what it is, what it wants, and what it fears — but the truth costs you: mark 1 Harm or a Condition from the strain of knowing.
- **Mentor.** When you steady or instruct an ally, they take +1 on their next roll.

**Dilettante** — *emphasize Presence.*
- ★ **Connections & Resources.** When you leverage money, status, or contacts, on a hit the door opens; on a 10+ it opens *and* you're owed a favor in return.
- **Unflappable.** When you Sway to calm or reassure, treat a 6− as a 7–9 once per scene.

**Survivor** — *emphasize Grace or Might.*
- ★ **Still Breathing.** When you flee, hide, or endure to stay alive, on a hit you make it; on a 10+ you also spot a way to turn the tables. On a miss, you survive — but at a real cost (Harm, a Scar, or cornered).
- **Adrenaline.** Once per session, push (take a Condition for +1) without the usual limit — twice on one roll for +2.

**Occultist** — *emphasize Resolve.*
- ★ **Ward & Rite.** When you use occult practice to protect, banish, or bind, roll + Resolve. On 10+ it holds. On 7–9 it holds, but choose: it costs you (Harm/Condition), it's temporary, or it demands something more. On a miss, you've drawn the wrong attention.
- **Iron Mind.** When you Endure a supernatural terror with Resolve, on a 7–9 you take no Condition (still any Harm).

## Threat roster

> **The Stalked Feeling (Minor, 1 Harm — atmospheric)** — Tags: *Unseen, Wrong.* Signature: *On a miss, it's closer than you thought — mark Shaken.* Instinct: *Herd them toward the real threat.* (Use to build dread before anything shows itself.)

> **Cultists (Standard, 3 Harm)** — Tags: *Fanatical, Numbers, Knives.* Signature: *On your miss, they complete a step of the rite — tick the ritual Clock.* Instinct: *Open the way for their god.*

> **The Possessed (Standard, 3 Harm)** — Tags: *Wears a familiar face, Unnatural strength, Says things it shouldn't know.* Signature: *On your cost/miss, it says a true, terrible thing — Endure with Resolve or mark Afraid.* Instinct: *Torment, then consume.*

> **Revenant / Slasher (Elite, 5 Harm)** — Tags: *Relentless, Can't be reasoned with, Comes back.* Signature: *It never stops; a miss means it's suddenly right there — take 2 Harm or a Scar. "Killing" it only buys time.* Instinct: *Punish the living, endlessly.*

> **The Thing in the Walls (Elite, 5 Harm)** — Tags: *Amorphous, Grasping, Feeds on fear.* Signature: *It strikes from any dark opening; each round anyone Afraid takes 1 Harm.* Instinct: *Drag prey into the dark and keep them.*

> **The Old One's Herald (Boss, 5 Harm + 6-Clock "The Veil Thins")** — Tags: *Sanity-eroding, Impossible geometry, Beyond death.* Signature: *Merely perceiving it clearly forces an Endure + Resolve or mark a Condition; on your miss, tick The Veil Thins. When it fills, the door between worlds opens.* Instinct: *Prepare the way; unmake what it touches.*

## Lenses

**Cosmic** *(signature — default dials).* Lovecraftian: vast indifferent powers, forbidden knowledge, sanity as a resource you spend to understand. *Dials: Weirdness 3.* Emphasize Resolve-as-sanity; truths cost Conditions; victories are partial and temporary.

**Gothic / Supernatural.** Ghosts, curses, old bloodlines, haunted places with rules you must learn. *Dials: Weirdness 2, Magic/Tech 1.* The Occultist shines; threats have specific banes and unfinished business.

**Survival / Slasher.** A relentless, physical threat and a shrinking cast; the horror is *being hunted.* *Dials: Lethality 3, Weirdness 1.* Favor the Survivor; use Clocks for the hunt; harm and Scars come fast.

**Psychological.** Is any of it real? Unreliable reality, paranoia, the threat that might be in your own head. *Dials: Weirdness 2, Lethality 1.* Blur what rolls reveal; use Conditions like *Paranoid* and *Unmoored*; let the player doubt.
